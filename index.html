Coshish

Coshish was formed by Mangesh in 2006 and is the creative engine of the band. In 2013, Coshish came out with their debut album called ‘Firdous’ under Universal music and received critical acclaim. Firdous reached #8 on iTunes, #28 on Nokia Music and set a record number of pre-orders on three websites.

Mangesh sings and plays the guitar along with writing lyrics and composing for Coshish.

Firdous Album

Coshish was nomited for Rolling Stone Jack Daniel’s Rock Awards 2013: Best Band, Best Album and Best Drummer for their album Firdous in 2013.

Coshish won Rolling Stone Jack Daniel’s Rock Awards 2013: Best Drummer (Hamza Kazi) for their song Maya in 2013.
